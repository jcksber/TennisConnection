Bronze. For the late bloomers.

  • Senior year expedited college recruiting package
  • College recruiting video
  • College coach correspondence
  • College application/essay assistance
  • Tournament/showcase schedule

Silver. It's okay to be a little behind!

  • Junior and senior year recruiting package
  • Comprehensive academic analysis
  • Player Overview created and player/coach correspondence established
  • Senior summer training schedule and college visit schedule

Gold. Take control of your recruiting process early.

  • Sophomore, junior and senior year recruiting package
  • Contains contents of bronze and silver packages
  • Also includes on and off court evaluations
  • Fitness scheduling and training regiment provided
  • Additional video analysis for stroke and match play

Platinum. For the prepared and eager (recommended).

  • Freshman through senior year recruiting package
  • Contains all contents of bronze through gold
  • Emphasis placed on understanding early phases of recruiting process
  • Importance of class selection discussed
  • Paves the way for easier college placement process down the road
